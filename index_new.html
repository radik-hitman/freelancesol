About Us
Freelance AI is designed to empower freelancers & clients by removing obstacles that hinder their success, ensuring that they can focus on delivering & receiving high-quality work without excessive fees or delays.
Leveraging decentralized finance (DeFi) and smart contracts, Freelance AI eliminates the need for third-party intermediaries, ensuring lower fees, faster payouts, & complete transparency. The platform provides essential features like customizable escrow contracts, integrated payments, & a simple, user-friendly interface, making freelancing more efficient & accessible.

Revenue & Growth

FreelanceAI is transforming the freelancing landscape with an innovative approach to revenue generation and growth. Unlike traditional platforms such as Upwork and Fiverr, which charge high transaction fees of 10-20%, FreelanceAI offers a significantly more competitive fee structure:

• 1-1.5% for clients
• 3-6% for freelancers

This low-cost model not only empowers users but also drives platform adoption. With an ambitious goal of onboarding 5,000 active users in the first year, FreelanceAI is projected to generate approximately $960,000 annually from transaction fees—a fraction of its total potential.

To further accelerate growth, FreelanceAI will reinvest revenue into strategic buybacks, strengthening token value and driving chart growth.
